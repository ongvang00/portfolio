My Interest

I enjoy traveling. I try to take at least two overseas trip a year. I love experiencing new cultures. Traveling can be fun however very tiring. People only show the glamorous side of traveling and never the ugly. Some ugly sides of traveling are managing a budget, waking up early to catch a bus, getting scam, and etc. However, I view it as an art form. Navigating a country where I do not speak the language can be beautiful in many ways. Every time I travel to a country that I dont speak the language, I empathize my parents who left everything they knew to come to America. A country they did not speak the language but built an entire life in it.
